Roy Aftimos

CEO

What drives me to keep pushing is the collective hunger of the tribe, and a relentless need to become a better version of who we are. Day in, day out.

An obsession with creating unignorable work is the motto of every day. And with more than two decades of strategic communication experience, I am on a mission to explore who we are as C2 and where we can be beyond the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and India.

What continually motivates us is our commitment to leveraging curiosity and technology to empower businesses to thrive.

When I am not looking into numbers, you will find me sailing, enjoying a Cuban, or reading through a national geographic magazine.

I am Roy and I am the CEO of C2.

Samer Qaqish

General Manager

Hello, I’m Samer Qaqish, General Manager and the go-to man who always gets things done. Policies, budgets, and all the business end of things that make our work unmissable rests on my shoulders - no pressure.

Speaking of pressure, it’s truly our teamwork that pulls us through each day. It is a collaborative effort paying off with steady growth and success while always taking the high road on quality.

To keep my team motivated, I prioritize the empowerment and encouragement of every individual, ensuring that each voice is heard and valued.

My motto has always been that a new day brings a new challenge. It’s the perfect setup to go the distance, stretch our creativity, and help create work that always turns heads.

Zahir Mirza

Executive Creative Director

It’s sometimes hard to talk about yourself in a way that doesn’t sound a bit conceited. But I’m going to give it my best shot anyway. Although I will fail.

Now I know you’ve probably heard this before from folks in the business but I’m an ideas guy. I just love them. Can’t get enough of them. Finding interesting things to say and then saying them in the most interesting way. That’s what I love about this business.

I wield the power of insights, the craft of storytelling and at times the wizardry of tech to help brands immerse themselves in pools of pop culture.

Over the years I’ve put a lot of hustle, heart and smarts in helping create a body of work that has been recognized at every major international awards show like the D&AD, One Show, Cannes Lions, Clios, Andy Awards, ADC, LIA, New York Festivals, Dubai Lynx & Effies.

I truly believe in the power and beauty of collaboration. That good heads around a table make better, so much better.

So let’s have some fun.

Together.

Robert Habib

Head of Strategy and Digital

Hey, I'm Robert Habib, but everyone at C2 calls me "Robs" or even "Robbie" when they're feeling nostalgic. I'm the guy who helps C2 with all things strategic. I've been loving everything about brands for a whole ten years, and guess what? All those ten years, I've been right here at C2!

Layal Elsayed

Business Director

Hey there, I’m Layal, your friendly neighborhood marketing guru, with 15 years of experience in global advertising. Think of me as your creative sidekick, always armed with some thought-provoking ideas. My superpower? Team development. I'm all about leading diverse groups to discover what makes consumers tick. Brand strategy? A breeze.

Social media and digital marketing? I eat algorithms for breakfast. Advertising? I can sell sand in the desert. But hold on, there's more! My retail strategy game is strong, my integrated marketing skills are legendary, and when it comes to innovation and new product development, I can make Elon Musk look like an intern.

My awards? You might need sunglasses! In a world of one-size-fits-all solutions, I’m your marketing magician, ready to make your brand shine! When I'm not in marketing mode, you'll find me making music, exploring new corners of the world, and giving fashion advice.

Taniya

HR Director

Hey there! I'm Tanya, but you can call me T – I'm a globe-trotting, non-traditional HR guru who splits her time between the vibrant cities of Dubai and Beirut.

Picture me as the Sherlock Holmes of the workplace, diving deep into the intricate minds of our team members.

They call me the 'shrink' around here, and I wear that badge with pride. With 28 years of experience navigating management and Human Resources in the Media & Communication industries, I'm your go-to gal for everything from recruiting and staffing to counseling and advice-giving.

But wait, there's more! I'm on a mission to make our workplace the absolute best, focusing on employee orientation, development, and training, all while promoting diversity and inclusion. Safety? Check. Welfare? Double-check. Wellness and health? Triple-check. Buckle up because we're about to redefine the HR game!

Nair

Finance Director

They say I'm the Holy Grail of numbers, and I've been part of the C2 family since way back in 1997. My journey in advertising began in the '80s when I first dipped my toes into the financial waters at Lintas Group in Dubai.

Today, I'm a master of Advertising Finance, armed with decades of financial wisdom and operational know-how. I'm no ordinary number cruncher; I'm a seasoned pro with a knack for making sense of those daunting spreadsheets. I’m able to sharply cut through the financial noise and bring clarity to any operation.

But, I'm not all about the numbers. I've also got a talent for striking that perfect balance between people and profits. So, if you're looking for a financial wizard with a dash of wit and a heart full of wisdom, you've found your guy.
